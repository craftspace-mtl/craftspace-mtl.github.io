Decorate your home

Here's a basic overview of what you need to know about interior design and decorating elements:

Color is one of the most important aspects of any design. Color can not only establish the mood for a space, it can also help to establish continuity in an environment. Entire courses are based solely on color, and it is important that designers have at least a cursory knowledge of the characteristics and roles of color. Interior decorators need to have an in-depth knowledge of color.

Texture creates depth and visual appeal in a space.

Interior decorating projects require that decorators incorporate various textures into their spaces. Texture helps to create mood, visual appeal, and depth. For example, paint that is applied with a Tuscan texture may help to evoke daydreams of Italy and all that those daydreams entail. Texture can be created through a variety of tools, including wall and furniture paint, wall coverings, upholstery, accessories, flooring, and more. Here is a brief overview about how each of these tools can be used to incorporate texture:

Paint

Designers use many elements to create texture, including paint finishes, upholstery, wall coverings, and accessories.

Wall Coverings

Wall coverings create depth, visual appeal, and contribute to the style of a space.
